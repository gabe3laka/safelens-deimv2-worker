# SafeLens Training Materials

These placeholder-driven templates turn approved, anonymized HSE evidence into
reviewable learning material.

## Templates

- `toolbox-talk-template.md`: facilitated team discussion
- `micro-learning-template.md`: short self-contained lesson
- `quiz-template.md`: knowledge check
- `supervisor-briefing-template.md`: shift/supervisor action brief
- `worker-safety-card-template.md`: concise field reminder
- `training-record-template.md`: attendance and competency record
- `before-after-explanation-template.md`: verified control comparison
- `refresher-training-template.md`: retraining after change, trend, or expiry
- `method-statement-summary-template.md`: concise task sequence, controls, permits, and emergency actions

## Rules

- Use anonymized evidence only.
- Remove faces, names, badges, plates, screens, and customer identifiers.
- Confirm content against the current company procedure and jurisdiction.
- Treat generated material as a preview until an authorized HSE reviewer approves it.
- Do not infer blame or disciplinary conclusions from model output.
